Our Story

They were never supposed to be called Sockets but after showing around old designs and sketches it led to everyone saying they look like something straight out of a wall.

Socket Face Club was started as a community driven project, members got to decide on themes and potential designs. Now, there are just 1.000 Socket NFTs made from over 300 traits scattered around 10 themes and layers!

We work hard to bring the best we have to the table. Our community members mean everything to us so by making you happy, we are. If you are looking for a chill place to hang out, play games and interact with people from all around the world, you are in the right place. We have big plans for this community and want to expand more and more for the following years.

Events and collabs are becoming more frequent with fun activities and giveaways. We don't just go around random communities, but try to set up our partnerships for the future. Also, as the look of the project has some history we don't want to pop up as just another copycat of a project.
